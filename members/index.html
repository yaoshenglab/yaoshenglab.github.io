    Principle Investigator:姚晟（Sheng Yao）

    Sheng Yao received the B.E. degree in mechatronics engineering and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from the South China University of Technology, Guangzhou, China, in 2016 and 2021, respectively. He was a joint Ph.D. Student at the Department of Mechanical and Industrial Engineering, University of Toronto, Toronto, ON, Canada, from 2018 to 2019. He is currently an associate professor with the School of Biomedical Engineering, Southern Medical University, Guangzhou. His research interests include high-precision vision systems and vision-based control for micromanipulation, with applications to cell surgery.

    南方医科大学生物医学工程学院副教授，硕士生导师。2016年毕业于华南理工大学，获工学学士； 2021年毕业于华南理工大学，获工学博士学位；2018至2019年曾在加拿大多伦多大学做联合培养博士。2021年6月加入南方医科大学生物医学工程学院工作至今，历任讲师、副教授。 主要的研究兴趣为面向细胞手术的微操作机器人感知与操控技术，主持国自然青年项目、广东省自然科学基金项目、广州市科技计划面上项目、广东省医学科研基金等各级科研项目。
